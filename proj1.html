Fun Facts

I grew up in Littleton, Colorado my entire eighteen years of existence. I grew up fifteen minutes away from Red Rocks and about an hour and a half from the best ski mountains in the west. Growing up, the summers were spent at Red Rocks seeing concerts and the winters were spent in the mountains skiing.

I went to Columbine High School all four years. It was the best community I could have possibly grown up in. The impact that going to Columbine is something I wouldn't trade for the world. The school was like a family. I made the best friends in the world and they became my second family.
