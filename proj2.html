Fun Facts

Growing up fifteen minutes away from Red Rocks (the coolest concert venue in the world) made it extremely easy for me to see shows. I've seen about twenty concerts at Red Rocks. I love going to shows with my friends in the summer. The atmosphere at Red Rocks is incredible. Everyone is so happy and nice. It's also the most beautiful place ever to see shows. The view of Red Rocks looks over the city of Denver and right as the show starts the sun goes down and the sunsets are always gorgeous. One of the things I miss most about Colorado is Red Rocks.
